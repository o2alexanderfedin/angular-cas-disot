What is CAS/DISOT?

CAS/DISOT is a decentralized content management system that combines content-addressable storage (CAS) with cryptographic signatures to create an immutable source of truth (DISOT).

How It Works

1

Upload Content

Upload any file to the system. It will be hashed using SHA-256 and stored by its content hash.

2

Create DISOT Entry

Sign your content with a cryptographic key to create a timestamped, verifiable entry.

3

Share & Verify

Share your signed entries. Anyone can verify the authenticity and timestamp of your content.

Technical Details

Content Addressing

  • SHA-256 hashing ensures unique content identification
  • Automatic deduplication - identical files share the same hash
  • Content integrity verification built-in

Storage Options

  • In-Memory: Fast temporary storage (default)
  • IndexedDB: Persistent browser storage
  • IPFS: Distributed storage (coming soon)

Cryptography

  • secp256k1 key pairs for digital signatures
  • Timestamp included in signed entries
  • Signature verification for authenticity
